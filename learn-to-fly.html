Learn To Fly Programs

explore the programs below to get more involved in aviation!

For Kids

Youth Eagles Program

In this program, children between 8 and 17 years old can fly in a small plane with a volunteer pilot for about 20 minutes, for free.

Aviation Summer Camp

EAA offers many summer camps for students in middle and high school, including ones hosted in the EAA Air Academy Lodge and EAA Aviation Museum.

Virutal Cockpit Tour

Explore the airplanes in the EAA Aviation Museum virtually through this site.

For Adults

Flying Clubs

This site connects you to flying clubs in your area and resources to start your own flying club.

Learn To Fly

AOPA provides tons of resources on how to get started with flying, whether recreationally or professionally. It also has information for active pilots about the latest aviation saftey programs.

Internships

Discover internship and career opportunities at EAA.
